How to Use RustForge

Load an Image

Use the main RustForge window to load your desired image by clicking "Load Image...".

Capture Your Regions (Press Enter after setting Canvas/Palette areas to save)

Palette: Use hotkey (F10 default) to draw a box around the in-game color palette. (ONLY CAPTURE THE COLOR PALETTE AREA)

Canvas: Use hotkey (F9 default) to draw a box around the in-game canvas where painting will occur.

Configure Settings

In the settings window, select your preferred paint method, apply filters, and adjust paint speed.

Start Painting

Once processed and regions defined, press the "Start" hotkey (F11 default) to begin.

Pause and Stop

Use assigned hotkeys (F11 for pause, Escape for stop) to control painting.
