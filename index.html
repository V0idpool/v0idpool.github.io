Premium Features

Premium unlocks advanced functionality and directly supports ongoing development.

**VoidWeave Algorithm:** The most advanced painting method for high detail and color accuracy.

**Resume Painting:** Press **F5** and click the canvas to resume from the exact row where you left off.

Core Features

**Quality & Performance**
  • **Background Color Ignore:** Skip painting solid background colors to save time.
  • **CIEDE2000 Color Space:** Choose the most perceptually accurate color matching method.
  • **Transparency Threshold:** Skip pixels based on their alpha transparency.
  • **Multiple Painting Methods:** Scanline, Crayon Eater, and VoidWeave (Premium).
**Utility & Experience**
  • **Load Recent Files:** Quickly access the last 10 images you used.
  • **Real-time Notifications:** Toast notifications for session status (Start/Pause/Stop).
  • **Integrated Image Filters:** Apply Comic, Oil Paint, Pixel Art, Sepia, and more.
  • **Configurable Hotkeys:** Customize Start, Pause, Stop, and Region Capture actions.
