How to Use RustForge

  • Load an Image: Use the main RustForge window to load your desired image by clicking "Load Image...".
  • Capture Your Regions: (Press Enter after setting Canvas/Palette areas to save)
    • Palette: Use hotkey (F10 default) to draw a box around the in-game color palette. (ONLY CAPTURE THE COLOR PALETTE AREA)
    • Canvas: Use hotkey (F9 default) to draw a box around the in-game canvas where painting will occur.
  • Configure Settings: In the settings window, select your preferred paint method, apply filters, and adjust paint speed.
  • Start Painting: Once processed and regions defined, press the "Start" hotkey (F11 default) to begin.
  • Pause and Stop: Use assigned hotkeys (F11 for pause, Escape for stop) to control painting.

‚å®Ô∏è Default Hotkeys

  • F9: Canvas Overlay Toggle - Toggles the canvas selection overlay.
  • F10: Palette Overlay Toggle - Toggles the palette selection overlay.
  • F11: Start/Pause - Starts or pauses the painting process.
  • Escape: Stop - Immediately stops the painting process.
  • F5: Resume On Click - Click a point on the canvas to resume from that row.

Hotkeys are configurable in the settings window.
